Today, iTabs has evolved into a versatile system for enhancing any digital content with contextual information and interactive elements. We've expanded beyond donation forms to create a platform that transforms how people interact with information online.

Our new approach centers around the familiar information icon i

The Information Icon

The lowercase "i" in a circle is universally recognized as the symbol for additional information, used across digital and physical interfaces worldwide.

Traditional vs. iTabs

Traditional "i": Simple tooltip with limited text.

iTabs "i": Rich, tabbed interface with multiple content types, media, and interactive elements.

- a symbol everyone recognizes, but that traditionally delivers disappointing results. We've reimagined what happens when you click that "i" - instead of a simple tooltip, users get rich, organized information that keeps them in context.
