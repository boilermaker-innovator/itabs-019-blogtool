What Are iTabs?

iTabs is a system that enhances digital content with rich, contextual information displayed in a tabbed interface, allowing users to access deeper content without losing their place.

About The Name

The name "iTabs" combines the information icon ("i") with the tabbed interface that organizes content, reflecting our approach to structured information delivery.

Traditional vs. iTabs

Traditional "i": Simple tooltip with limited text.
